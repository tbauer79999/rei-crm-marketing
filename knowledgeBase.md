## WHAT IT DOES

SurFox AI is an AI SDR platform that conducts full back-and-forth SMS and web chat conversations with leads, qualifies intent, scores them on a 50-point scale, and surfaces hot leads to the sales team. Human involvement only happens when a lead is ready to talk.

**Core flow:**
1. **Upload Leads** — Import thousands of cold leads in bulk via CSV, spreadsheet, or API integration
2. **AI Engages** — SurFox AI starts personalized SMS conversations with each lead via Twilio
3. **Scores & Qualifies** — AI scores each conversation on a 50-point scale, identifying hot prospects
4. **Sales Handoff** — When a lead hits "hot" status, the sales team gets notified to take over
