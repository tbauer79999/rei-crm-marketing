# SurFox AI Positioning Document v1

## Category
SMS Lead Qualification

## Positioning Statement
SurFox AI activates your contact list and finds the buyers before your competition knows they exist.

## Two Motions
1. **Cold list activation** -- upload thousands of contacts, AI works every one
2. **Speed to lead** -- new inbound leads contacted in seconds automatically

## The Buyer
Small business owner or sales leader who either has a cold list they've never fully worked, or is losing inbound leads to slow follow-up. No SDR team. Needs the AI to do the work.

## Verticals
- **Primary:** Real estate investors and wholesalers
- **Secondary:** Staffing and healthcare staffing
- **Third (6 months):** Mortgage and insurance
- **Watch list (60 days):** Home services -- speed to lead motion only

## Core Pain
Leads go cold. Lists go unworked. Revenue sits untouched.

## Who We Educate
Text blasters who think volume is the answer. They feel the pain but think more volume is the fix. We show them conversation beats broadcast.

## Who We Are NOT For
B2B cold outbound teams using email and LinkedIn.

## The Gap We Own
Real conversational AI at SMB pricing. Not $1,000/month. Not enterprise contracts. Not a chatbot.

## What We Are NOT
- An AI SDR platform
- A text blaster
- A B2B cold email tool
- A LinkedIn outreach tool
- A chatbot
