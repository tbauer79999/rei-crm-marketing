# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: March 16, 2026*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** SurFox AI qualifies your SMS leads automatically so you stop paying humans to follow up.
**What it does:** SurFox AI is an AI-native SMS platform that engages, qualifies, and books leads via text message -- autonomously. The AI handles the entire conversation with the goal of getting a lead on the phone, learning from every exchange to get sharper over time.
**Product category:** AI SDR platform / SMS lead qualification
**Product type:** SaaS
**Business model:** Subscription, tiered by message volume. Starter $97/1K messages, Growth $497/10K messages, Scale $1,997/40K messages. 14-day free trial.

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Real estate wholesalers (beachhead ICP); staffing agencies (established vertical); healthcare adjacent
**Decision-makers:** Owner/operator (wholesalers are typically solo or small team); operations director or VP (staffing)
**Primary use case:** Replacing manual SMS follow-up on inbound and outbound leads
**Jobs to be done:**
- Follow up with every lead instantly without hiring an SDR or VA
- Qualify leads at scale without being glued to a phone
- Get more leads booked on the phone without more headcount

**Use cases:**
- Real estate wholesaler texting motivated seller lists
- Staffing agency qualifying job applicants via SMS
- Any high-volume outbound SMS operation with manual follow-up bottleneck

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** High-volume SMS lead follow-up is manual, inconsistent, and doesn't scale. Leads go cold while operators are busy.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- SmarterContact and similar tools are SMS delivery platforms -- they send texts but don't have real AI conversation capability. Humans still do the qualifying.
- Hiring VAs or SDRs adds payroll, training, and inconsistency
- Generic AI chatbots aren't trained on sales conversations and don't know when to push vs. back off

**What it costs them:**
- Time: Hours per day manually texting and following up
- Money: $800+/month for tools that still require human labor on top
- Deals: Leads go cold between follow-ups; speed to lead is a direct driver of conversion

**Emotional tension:** "I'm paying for a texting tool and still doing all the work myself." Overwhelm from volume. Fear of missing deals while they're handling other things.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** SmarterContact -- falls short because it's an SMS delivery tool, not an AI. Humans still qualify every lead. $800/month for 20K messages with no intelligence layer.
**Secondary:** Hiring VAs or SDRs -- falls short because it's expensive, inconsistent, requires training, and doesn't scale without adding more headcount.
**Indirect:** Doing it manually themselves -- falls short because operators can't be available 24/7, leads go cold, and it doesn't scale past a certain volume.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Genuine AI conversation engine -- not canned responses, actual intent classification and adaptive replies
- Conversation learning by plan (Growth: last 100 conversations, Scale: last 1,000) -- gets smarter over time
- Built-in referral system
- Zapier integration (live in public marketplace)
- Google/Outlook calendar OAuth for direct booking
- Knowledge base document upload -- AI knows your business, not just generic scripts
- Migrated to Anthropic Claude for better conversation quality

**How we do it differently:** The AI runs the entire conversation autonomously with the goal of booking a call -- not just sending a message and waiting for a human to take over.
**Why that's better:** Operators get leverage. Every lead gets followed up instantly, consistently, at any hour, without adding headcount.
**Why customers choose us:** They're paying for SMS tools that still require manual work. SurFox AI removes the human from the qualification loop entirely.

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Will it sound robotic?" | The AI is trained to converse naturally. Customers have mistaken it for a human rep. You control the persona and tone. |
| "I already use SmarterContact" | SmarterContact sends texts. SurFox AI qualifies leads. They're not the same category -- you still need a human on top of SmarterContact. |
| "What if the AI says something wrong?" | You upload your knowledge base and control the persona. The AI stays in scope. You can monitor conversations in real time. |
| "I'm not sure I have enough volume" | Starter plan at $97/month is low risk. If you're sending more than a few hundred texts a month manually, the ROI is immediate. |

**Anti-persona:** Companies that want full human control over every message. Businesses with very low lead volume where automation doesn't move the needle. Enterprise teams requiring complex procurement cycles (for now).

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Frustrated paying $800/month for a tool that still requires manual follow-up. Tired of leads going cold. Can't scale without hiring.
**Pull:** Full automation of the qualification loop. Pay for messages, not headcount. Gets smarter over time. Easy setup via Zapier.
**Habit:** Already trained their team on SmarterContact workflows. Comfortable with the current tool even if it's inefficient.
**Anxiety:** "What if the AI messes up a conversation and I lose a deal?" Fear of losing the personal touch that closes deals in real estate.
